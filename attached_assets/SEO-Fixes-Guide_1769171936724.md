## 1. ✅ CRITICAL: Add H1 Tag to Homepage

### Current Code (index.html - Hero Section)
```html
<h2>One Step Innovations</h2>
<h2>Transform business chaos into streamlined efficiency</h2>
```

### Fixed Code
```html
<h1>One Step Innovations | AI Automation & IoT Solutions for Manufacturing</h1>
<h2>Transform business chaos into streamlined efficiency</h2>
```

**Why:** Google needs the H1 tag to understand your page's primary topic. Currently, it sees no H1 = no clarity on what the page is about.

**Time to fix:** 30 seconds

## 3. 🟠 MEDIUM: Update Meta Descriptions

### Current Meta Descriptions (Too Short)

**Homepage:**
```html
<meta name="description" content="Transform business chaos into streamlined efficiency...">
```

**Services Page:**
```html
<meta name="description" content="From AI Process Automation to IoT Solutions...">
```

**Contact Page:**
```html
<meta name="description" content="Get in touch with One Step Innovations...">
```

### Optimized Meta Descriptions (150-160 chars each)

**Homepage:**
```html
<meta name="description" content="Transform business chaos into efficiency with One Step Innovations. AI Process Automation, IoT solutions, and Strategic Management for Australian enterprises.">
```

**Services Page:**
```html
<meta name="description" content="Expert AI Process Automation, IoT Solutions, and Strategic Management services for SME and enterprise manufacturers across Australia. Proven results.">
```

**Contact Page:**
```html
<meta name="description" content="Contact One Step Innovations for AI & IoT consulting in Australia. Expert team ready to transform your manufacturing business. Call or email today.">
```

**Why:** Meta descriptions appear under your URL in Google search results. They should be:
- 150-160 characters (current ones are ~80-90)
- Include relevant keywords (IoT, AI, Australia, manufacturing)
- Compelling (encourages clicks from search results)

**Time to fix:** 3 minutes

**Impact:** Better Click-Through Rate (CTR) from search results = higher rankings
